KERELA

Kerala, a state located in the southwestern region of India, is often referred to as "God's Own Country" for its lush greenery, serene backwaters, and vibrant cultural heritage. Blessed with a diverse landscape that includes beautiful beaches, tranquil backwaters, rolling hills adorned with tea and spice plantations, and dense forests, Kerala offers a unique blend of natural beauty.

Uttarakhand, often referred to as the "Land of the Gods," is a picturesque state nestled in the northern part of India. This enchanting region was carved out of the northern part of Uttar Pradesh and officially became a separate state in the year 2000. Uttarakhand is renowned for its stunning landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and spiritual significance.
